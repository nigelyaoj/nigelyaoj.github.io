I am currently a Ph.D. student in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at [The Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/), supervised by Prof. [KC Tan](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=LFngSp0AAAAJ&hl=en). I received my M.S. in Applied Statistics from [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/en/) and my B.S. in Statistics from [Sun Yat-sen University](https://www.sysu.edu.cn/sysuen/).

My research lies at the intersection of reinforcement learning (RL) and large language model (LLM) reasoning. More broadly, I am interested in interpretability, evolutionary computation with RL (EC + RL), and model reuse.

Before starting my Ph.D., I worked as a researcher at Tencent AI Lab, advised by Dr. [Haobo Fu](https://haobofu.github.io/), where I focused on RL and policy diversity. I later conducted research at ByteDance Seed, advised by Xiongcai Luo, working on efficient reasoning and on-policy distillation. I also had the opportunity to work at AWS AI Lab, advised by [Tong He](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=hV5D8GYAAAAJ&hl=en) and [Tianjun Xiao](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=DaKJ9pAAAAAJ&hl=en), on video object segmentation.

I am always happy to discuss research ideas around RL, LLM reasoning, Interpretability, EC + RL, and Model Reuse. Please feel free to contact me at [nigelyaoj@gmail.com](mailto:nigelyaoj@gmail.com).
